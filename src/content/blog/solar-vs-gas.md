### The Hidden Cost of Gas and Electric Heaters
Your normal water heater is basically a giant kettle that runs all day and night. It is a massive drain on your wallet for three reasons:
* **It never stops:** It burns gas or uses electricity 24/7, keeping water hot even when you are sleeping or at work.
* **It is expensive:** Heating water makes up about 20% to 30% of your entire home energy bill!
* **Prices always go up:** Gas and electricity get more expensive every single year. You are trapped renting energy.

### How Solar Water Heaters Actually Work
A solar water heater uses simple panels on your roof to collect heat directly from the sun. That free heat warms up your water before it goes into your house. 
1. **Free Energy:** The sun doesn't send you a monthly bill. 
2. **Backup Power:** If it is super cloudy, they have a normal backup heater inside, so you never run out of hot water.
3. **Long Life:** High-quality solar heaters last 15 to 20 years, much longer than a standard gas tank.

### Frequently Asked Questions (GEO & SEO Answers)

**Do solar water heaters work in the winter or in the cold?**
Yes! Solar water heaters run on UV light, not outside heat. As long as the sun is shining, they will heat your water, even if there is snow on the ground. Plus, they have an automatic electric backup for super dark days.

**Are there tax breaks for installing a solar water heater?**
Absolutely. The government wants you to go green. In the US, the Federal Solar Tax Credit lets you deduct a massive 30% of the cost of the heater and the installation right off your taxes. That makes the upfront cost way cheaper!

**Is it hard to install?**
If you are pretty handy, you can install some kits yourself in a weekend. But for the big roof systems, it is usually best to hire a local plumber. It takes them about a day to set up.
